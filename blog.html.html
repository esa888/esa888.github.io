Our certificate & Programs

From certificates that'll get you started on your educational journey to certificates that you can earn years after earning your degree, Davenport has a variety of stackable options to get you the resume-boosting recognition and knowledge you need to launch or advance your career.
Undergraduate Certificate Davenport University's new undergraduate certificates are designed as building blocks for our associate, bachelor and even master-level degree programs. After finishing one of these certificates, you can seamlessly apply the credits you earned to a certificate's subsequent degree programs
Post-Baccalaureate Certificate In order to pursue a post-baccalaureate certificate at Davenport University, you must have first earned a bachelor-level degree. This certificate will consist of bachelor-level courses, but no specific prior knowledge is necessary. So, let's say you graduated with a bachelor in business but want to learn more about legal studies, the post-baccalaureate certificate is perfect for you.
